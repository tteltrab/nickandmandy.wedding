Our Story

It All Started in High School

Nick and Mandy met in high school — though they didn't date back then. They both liked each other, but didn't act on it being foolish young teenagers.

Nick was a year ahead of Mandy, but she ended up going to UNC as well. They struck up a few conversations and went to more than a few parties together. They made it "Facebook official" in 2010, shortly before Nick deleted his Facebook. He now uses Mandy to keep in contact with everyone.

At this point, they've been together for about 6 and a half years. The relationship held strong through over 20 months of studying abroad and remote work. Over the years a lot has changed for them — they've had a few roommates, moved cities, changed jobs, and picked up a couple of pets. The only 'constant' for each of them was the other — and they'd like to keep it that way. So after 6 years they're finally getting married!
